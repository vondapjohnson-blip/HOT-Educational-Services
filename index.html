About HOT

Hazel Optics Technologies (HOT) partners with Alabama schools and families to improve student outcomes in math and literacy. We combine data, coaching, and family engagement to support growth for every learner.

Instructional Coaching & PD

Targeted coaching and professional learning for teachers focused on Tier 1 instruction, small-group routines, and effective use of assessment data.

Data & Assessment Support

Support with i-Ready, ACAP, and local assessments to identify needs, plan interventions, and monitor student progress over time.

Family Engagement Experiences

HOT Family Nights, take-home resources, and simple strategies that help families support learning at home in fun, practical ways.

HOT for Families: Home Opportunities for Thinking

We believe families are powerful partners. HOT provides simple, ready-to-use activities, games, and conversations that turn everyday moments into learning opportunities.
